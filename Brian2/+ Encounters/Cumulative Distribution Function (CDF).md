## Cumulative Distribution Function (CDF)


The probability that $X$ takes is less than or equal to $x$.
$$F_X(x)=P(X\le x)$$

The probability that $X$ lies in the semi-closed interval $(a,b]$.

$$P(a\lt X \le b)=F_x(b)-F_x(a)$$
