## Probability density function

The probability function $f(x)$ is defined such that:

$$
\int_a^b f(x)\,dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$
Remember that the [[Cumulative Distribution Function (CDF)]] is defined:

$$
F(a) = Pr(X <- a)
$$
