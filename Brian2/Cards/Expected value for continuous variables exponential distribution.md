## Expected value for continuous variables exponential distribution

$$
\begin{align}
\mbox{E}(X) &=\int\, x\, L(X=x)\; dx \\
&= \int_0^\infty\,\lambda x e^{-\lambda x}\, dx \\
&=\frac{1}{\lambda} 
\end{align}
$$
