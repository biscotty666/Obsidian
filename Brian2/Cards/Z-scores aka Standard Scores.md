## Z-scores aka Standard Scores

The number of standard deviations a sample is from the population mean.


### If population mean and standard deviation are known

$$
z=\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}
$$
### If unknown, use the sample mean and standard error
$$
z=\frac{x-\bar{x}}{S}
$$
