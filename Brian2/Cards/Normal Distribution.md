## Normal Distribution

It is symmetric and defined by a mean, $\mu$ and a [[Standard Deviation]], $\sigma$.

$$
f(x)=\frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{{2\pi}}}e^{-\frac{1}{2}(\frac{\sigma-\mu}{\sigma})^2}
$$
The distribution variance is $\sigma^2$ .
