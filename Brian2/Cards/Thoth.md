## Thoth

God of:
- Science, knowledge and writing
- Wisdom and judgement
- Art and magic
- Maintainer of the Universe ([[Ptah]] is the creator)
- Feminine counterpart is [[Seshat]]
- Wife was [[Maat]], godess of balance, order and harmony
